I am a fifth-year graduate student soon to receive my PhD from Princeton University's department of Chemical and Biological Engineering, with a Graduate Certificate in Computational and Information Science.

My research interests are in data mining and dimensionality reduction for high-dimensional dynamical systems, with applications in computational neuroscience. I also work on robotic design and perception projects.
